Welcome to iHeart



iHeart is a heart disease prediction system that uses neural networks to analyze key health data such as age, blood pressure, and cholesterol levels. It provides users with a risk assessment and personalized recommendations for preventing cardiovascular issues, enabling early detection and promoting heart health awareness.
